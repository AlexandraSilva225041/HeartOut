Design Elements

    • #1E1E1E Black is the first thing that comes to mind when we think of Hard Rock. It represents the darkness, intense feelings, extremes, and aesthetic value of the music genre. It's edgy and even scary. #1E1E1E is a slightly lighter black, making it easier to contrast.

    • #2E0038 Dark Purple goes along side the black on this one, as it is a powerful color frequently used in album covers and other rock artwork. It is dynamic and slightly unexpected.

    • #AE0909 The main value of HeartOut is Passion, and that is what this burning red represents. It is also heavily used in rock artwork.

    • #FF8000 This saturated yellow brings some contrast to the color scheme, differentiating it. It is also a great symbol of power, Energy and Community.

  • Font choices

    • Medieval Sharp - Header Font

    Medieval Sharp is a, as the name says, sharp and powerful font. It embodies some curves while keeping its energy and harshness. It is still readable. Various hard rock artoworks, such as album covers, include similar, medieval style fonts. This typeface is suitable for headers as it calls attention and adds personality to the brand.


    Roboto Regular - Body Type

    Roboto is a simple and readable typeface. Since the website has a dark background, which makes it more difficult to create contrast, as well as a considerable amount of text and detail, readability is very important.


    Justification of Navigation and UI patterns

    In the Home Page, the visitor gets an understanding of what they can do on the website, in other words, of the product, and what is the meaning of their actions. The power they hold by being able to help other people by writing a review, as well as checking other reviews. The second one is more encouraged (through a button) since it is a smoother way to engage witht the community at first. Since the beginning, it should be obvious for the visitor that HeartOut is not like most online community where people can share their interest, but rather a place where they can be helpful for each other, which connects to the USP. The containers help separate the copy in the page and the video-replayer makes sit more dynamic. (PUT VALUES IN THE COPY OF THIS PAGE)


    In the About Us page, the actual values of the brand are stated, and a better explanation of the brand, rather than product. It is more about the main idea of HeartOut. The separation of the product and brand has the intention of intensifying the idea of each one, instead of mixing them. The product may be the reviews website, but the brand is about the community and helping each other. There is also an introduction to the concept development team, bringing the visitors closer to the where the concept came from. It includes pictures of the members since, according to research, hard rock fans like face to face interactions and enjoy closeness. This page uses simple containers.


    The reviews are in a whole separate page, as it makes sense for them to be all together in one place. The cards create contrast between each event. By clicking the review the visitor can see it more in detail on a separate page, which helps focusing on one event at a time. Columns are used in the reviews part of the website, since it was the best way to divide the categories and the stars reviews while being aligned. Hard rock fans are often interested in specific artist or venues, so they can view the specific review categories only on those events. The button “Write Review” leads to a separate page where the visitor can focus only on this.


    According to the research conducted, hard rock fans do not like to chat online, and the communication works more on the basis of word of mouth. That is why this rating system works. They do not have to text or comment, but have that possibility. This way they are sure that the event they intent on going will match their expectations.


    The category ratings such as Performance, Vibes, Safety, Offline and Hard Hard Rock connect with the brand values of Passion, Loyalty, Safety, and Feeling Present in the Moment. Research showed that hard rock fans tend to not be on their phones during concerts and care a lot about the loud music while keeping its quality.

    Marketing

    Context of campaign and promotional activities

    After the multiple problem interviews we did as a team, we created a persona. From these outcomes, we found the category need of the target audience is people who want to share their passion with others. With these we created brand awareness, a platform called Heart Out, which is not just an overview of all events in one place but be able to find events that fit someone's value in a quick overview for reviewing. There are no obligations to interact with others but still possible. We based the values of the event review on insights from the problem interviews. What became part of our brand attitude are passion, loyalty, safety, and feeling in the moment. To be unique as a brand we strive from passion and have the ability to feel present in the moment, while still providing safety and security. With Heart Out we want to create a passionate community for hard rock fans of all ages who share the same values.


    Learning Points

    Are communication objective is based on the population of Noord Brabant and rock and alternative music listeners in Noord Brabant, according to our interview insights rock fans don’t usually follow much content on social media and do not engage or participate frequently with their music genre, therefore our goal is to raise engagement by a 1000 people with a response of interact on our social media page and share with word of mouth. Our media tactics are via Facebook and Instagram. We track our objective by comparing the views of a post and the likes.
    We first started with still single images that communicate our values and vision of Heart Out. We noticed we didn’t get much engagement in general therefore we posted a moving visual ´reel´ on Instagram and Facebook. After posting this went from an average of 21 views to 60 views on Instagram, in the statics that could be in the appendix there is a clear after-effect of posting the reel. To keep track of our objective we compare the number of views and likes. Although more people saw our reel on Instagram, as expected, they didn´t seek further interaction by liking the post. However, on Facebook we applied the same method there noticed the view stayed stable, didn’t change much and therefore didn’t have much effect.


    Future Planning

    In our communication planning is our biggest learning point not using the same strategy on two different platforms. We solve this by posting more frequently and different kinds of posts so there is more variety. Since we are a profit-making company, we start to work with sponsorship to increase the revenue stream and create a bigger awareness among the target audience. The objective is mention to raise awareness by word of mouth despite being difficult to track it is an important factor for our target audience. We will be focusing more on offline marketing, therefore hanging posters in local pubs, bars and pop stages. In this way, we with Heart Out trying to be closer to our main goal to share our passion for hard rock music with others.
