Who Is HeartOut?

HeartOut Vision

In a world full of judgements and society expecting you to stick to the norm, we believe that feeling passion and letting go are key, because only then we can be ourselves living in the moment while feeling safe and secure.


PASSION

Passion isn’t just a feeling. It is that one thing that charges your battery or the thing that you never stop to talking about. We with Heart Out have the same, for one exception we share it with you.

LOYALTY

At Heart Out, we don't just consider sound quality, safety and performance. We are there, for you and for the community to share our passion.

SAFETY

In a world of chaos, stress and expectation. Is safety a number priority Heart Out agrees. With Heart Out we help you to find the best event where safety is the number one priority for everyone.

PRESENT

You are not here. You are not there. You are now. Forget work. Forget the next week's meeting. With Heart out you live in the moment. Find your now moment, here and now.
